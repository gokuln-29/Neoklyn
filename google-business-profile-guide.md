# Google Business Profile Guide — NeoKlyn

## 1) Categories to Set (and Why)

### Primary Category: **Software Company**
Use this as primary because NeoKlyn's core commercial identity is engineering-led product development (AI systems, web platforms, mobile applications, and enterprise software delivery). Primary category strongly influences map relevance and search intent matching, so this should map to your highest-value buying intent.

### Secondary Category: **Web Design Company**
Use this as secondary to capture broader discovery for users searching visual-first terms such as "web design", "website redesign", and "UI/UX agency". This supports top-of-funnel discovery while preserving software positioning as primary.

## 3) Photo Strategy (What to Upload and Why)

### A. Brand Foundation (first uploads)
1. **Logo** (square, high-contrast)
2. **Cover Photo** (team + workstation or branded studio visual)
3. **Office Exterior/Building Photo** (if applicable)

Why: These establish trust and improve profile completion quality signals.

### B. Trust & Team Content (monthly cadence)
1. Team collaboration photos (real people, real workspace)
2. Founder/leadership portraits
3. Whiteboard planning sessions / sprint ceremonies
4. Client workshop snapshots (with permission)

Why: Demonstrates active operations and human credibility.

### C. Proof of Work Visuals
1. Before/after redesign snapshots
2. Dashboard/product UI screenshots
3. Performance metrics visuals (e.g., Lighthouse, conversion improvements)
4. Launch-day milestone images

Why: Adds evidence-backed differentiation over generic agency listings.

### D. Upload Standards
- Keep image style consistent (brand colors, clean composition)
- Prefer landscape for cover and updates
- Add 3–5 photos per month
- Avoid stock-heavy, text-overlaid images as primary proof assets

## 6) Website SEO Linking Recommendations

### A. Structured Data Alignment
- Keep `Organization` and `LocalBusiness` schema updated with consistent NAP
- Include same business phone/email used in GBP
- Add `sameAs` social profiles identical to GBP links

### B. NAP Consistency
Ensure **Name, Address, Phone** are identical across:
- Website footer/contact page
- GBP profile
- Major directories/social business profiles

Even minor differences can weaken local relevance.

### C. Review Widget / Social Proof
- Display recent Google reviews on `/` and `/contact`
- Add a "Read our Google Reviews" link to profile URL
- Use review snippets responsibly (no fake aggregation)

### D. Internal Linking for Local Intent
- Link from service pages to contact page with city mention where natural
- Add a short "Serving clients globally from Bengaluru" block on About and Contact

### E. Operational Cadence
- Post one GBP update weekly (project wins, insights, launches)
- Upload fresh photos monthly
- Request 2-4 new reviews per month with personalized context
